## The numbers from 10 to 20 are these:

*   10 deset
*   11 jedanaest
*   12 dvanaest
*   13 trinaest
*   14 četrnaest
*   15 petnaest
*   16 šesnaest
*   17 sedamnaest
*   18 osamnaest
*   19 devetnaest
*   20 dvadeset

These numbers always require the Genitive plural case for the following noun.

*   _Example:_ sedamnaest kuć__a__ (Genitive plural) - seventeen houses.
